# Mihrab — Product Context

**Product:** Mihrab
**Tagline:** "Where the Adhan Meets the Digital Age"
**Category:** Islamic community platform
**Geography:** India (nationwide)
**Audience:** Muslims across India — families, mosque administrators, youth, elderly community members
**Language:** English (primary UI), Arabic (prayer names, Quranic references)

## Core Value Propositions
1. Unified prayer time aggregation across all local masjids
2. Masjid directory with inline prayer time updates
3. Community blog and news
4. Business directory for Muslim-owned businesses
5. Multimedia gallery of community life

## Emotional Tone
- Sacred but not stuffy
- Modern but not cold
- Warm, trustworthy, precise
- Inspiring a sense of belonging and community

## Brand Personality
- Dignified: every pixel reflects the gravitas of the deen
- Warm: South Asian hospitality built into the visual language
- Precise: prayer times are sacred — the UI must feel accurate and reliable
- Inclusive: usable by elderly grandmothers and tech-savvy youth alike

## Platform Constraints
- No native app (Next.js web only, serves as the app's marketing/utility site)
- Server-rendered for SEO (App Router)
- Mobile-first (many users on Android mid-range devices)
- Performance matters — warm cream backgrounds over heavy image use

## Anti-References (what NOT to look like)
- Generic Muslim app templates with green and gold clip art
- Overly corporate fintech aesthetic
- Pure white + teal SaaS look
- Dark mode with neon accents
